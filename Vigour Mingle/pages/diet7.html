Healthy Chocolate Rice Bars
Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup chopped chocolate
  • 6 rice cakes
  • 1 tsp almond flour
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate with the coconut oil in the microwave in 20-30s intervals, until completely melted. Stir in between to speed this process up. It actually took me one interval of microwaving to melt the chocolate.

  2. Meanwhile, fry the leeks gently in the oil and butter for 5 mins or until soft. Add the fromage frais to the leeks and very gently warm through, stirring constantly to ensure it doesnâ€™t split. Add the herbs and steamed vegetables with a splash of pasta water to loosen.

  3. Drain the pasta and stir into the sauce. Adjust the seasoning, then serve scattered with the cheese and drizzled with a little extra olive oil.
